The Woman
Behind the Needle

J-Won Couture is the creative vision of Ogundimu Rokibat Olajuwon — a passionate fashion designer rooted in Abeokuta, Ogun State. With a deep love for African textile heritage and an eye for modern silhouettes, Rokibat has built a brand that celebrates the beauty of the African woman.

Every stitch at J-Won Couture tells a story — of culture, confidence, and craftsmanship. From intimate fittings to grand bridal reveals, we pour heart into every garment we create.
